Who Are We?

Khan's Cookies is a local cookie bakery in Carroll County Maryland.

Khan's Cookies was founded and envisioned by Zyad Khan who believed that everyone needs a cookie!

In order to give back to the community, Khan's Cookies pledges a whopping 0% of all sales to go to local organizations. This is because we make absolutely no money at all!
