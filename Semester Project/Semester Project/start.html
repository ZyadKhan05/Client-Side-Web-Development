Welcome to the Attorney's Firm

Attorney's is the product of collaboration between 6 attorneys whose goal is to provide legal advice and guidance to those who cannot afford
the high costing charges from other legal advisors.

Additionally Attorney's was founded for anyone who may just choose not to have representation at court when going through custody or
divorce proceedings.

Founded in 2022, the firm has the standard of delivering high quality services always at a low cost. Every case or trial is different, which is
why we provide the lawyer guidance and advice that is necessary for you. This includes lawyer guidance and advice through the litigation
process if you chose to represent yourself in court.


Why Should I Represent Myself In Court?

Attorney's believes that there are benefits to representing yourself in court which include:

  1. Having the ability to pick your main issues and make your own arguments
  2. The price to hire an attorney or lawyer to represent you may be high especially since cases may take a long time.
  3. You have the right to represent yourself if you choose so.
  4. Only you know the most about yourself, which is why it is truly up to you.

And no matter what, Attorney's will provide you with lawyer guidance and advice throughout the litigation process. If you need any immediate
assistance, please contact us and provide more information. We will be able to get a reply within 2 or 3 business days.
